Pandamonium

You can lead a panda to the bedroom but you can't make it mate!

Giant pandas are fast becoming extinct.
You look after the last 4 pandas. You need 20 to stabilise the population.
But they are very fussy. They'll only mate if they have something in common.
Click on a panda to find out about them. Double click two pandas to ask them to mate.
Your matchmaking skills are the only thing that can save the species.
But hurry, time is running out!
